Co-Op

The "Fun" and "challenging" Jobs


Software Developer + Business Analyst (B.A)

@ Genoix International Pvt Ltd.
Location: New Dehli, India

This was my first international experience.      I was given a wide variety of responsibilities:
- Developing the WeParcel website using {CSS} and{HTML} with an easy to modify platform for
non-developers and developers alike.
- Fixing
the WeParcel Application and creating grounds for its web app.
- Traveling
to various factories and managing the engineers there
- Organizing
business meetings and plans, producing legal terms and conditions draft for the
WeParcel App, creating a business plan for the next project, and providing the company with social media promotion strategies
and implementations.
This Co-op was a great experience for me since I learned to adapt well in any environment,
appreciate cultural differences, and learn new perspective in the engineering problem
solving environment. I happily worked overtime because I really loved what I
was doing. Not only was I able to reunite with my distant family, I was able to
enjoy great food and local entertainment. Given a chance in the late future
again I would love to work for this company again! I am currently
working as a contractor for their latest start up.






Android Developer

@ Banalogic
Location : Richmond Hill, Canada


This was my first co-op job. Coming into the job I had already mastered the basics of Java, android development, and possessed strong soft skills. Leaving the job I had mastered: Front-end and Back-end Android development using Java and C++; Crashlytics and deep mobile testing using Firebase; PHP token encryption authentication with UTC timestamp; Agile workflow; Git with Linux Command Line; Launching a beta-testing environment and publishing an app with legal parameters; Assembling a PC and modifying a Linux flavor to fit the developers needs. This Co-op was special to me because my supervisors were very kind and helpful. The environment they created encouraged me to work more than what was expected of me. I found the ticket system in Microsoft visual studio helped keep me competitive with the other co-op student to finish the most work. I would love to work here again because the wide variety of work kept me excited.
